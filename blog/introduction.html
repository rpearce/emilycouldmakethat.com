Introduction

Let’s be real, I don’t have a clue how to blog.

But here I am, giving it a go anyway. I have always had a passion for learning new skills and creating things. Whether it is picking up a craft I have never tried before or finding a new way to make something, I love the process of figuring it out and ending up with something I can hold in my hands.

This blog is my way of documenting those projects — the successes, the failures, and everything in between. I want to share what I learn along the way so that if you are curious about trying something new, you can follow along and maybe even give it a go yourself.

I am not an expert at any of this. I am just someone who sees something and thinks, “I could make that.” And then I try. Sometimes it works out brilliantly, and sometimes it does not. Either way, I will be sharing it here.

So welcome to emily could make that. I hope you enjoy following along.
