About

It all started when…

Let's be real, I don't have a clue how to blog. But I do love learning new skills and creating things. This site is my way of documenting creative projects and sharing what I learn along the way.

From macramé wall hangings and jewellery to paper flowers, book folding, and creative gift ideas — if I can make it, I want to share how you can too.

All of my tutorials are designed to be beginner-friendly, with easy step-by-step instructions. No prior experience needed!

You can also find my video tutorials on YouTube (opens in new tab) and craft kits in my Etsy shop (opens in new tab).
